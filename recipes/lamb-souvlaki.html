Lamb Souvlaki

Picture of a Lamb Souvlaki dish

Lamb souvlaki with marinated pieces of lamb, threaded on skewers, and char-grilled to perfection.
I like to serve these with rosemary garlic roasted potatoes, a Greek salad, and pita bread.

Ingredients

  • ⅓ cup olive oil
  • 1 ½ tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 ½ tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 ½ tablespoons chopped fresh oregano
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 ½ pounds boneless leg of lamb, trimmed of all fat and cut into 1-inch cubes

Steps

  1. Whisk olive oil, lemon juice, red wine vinegar, oregano, garlic, salt, and pepper
    together in a medium bowl. Add cubed lamb and stir until lamb is coated with marinade.
    Cover and refrigerate 3 hours, or overnight.

  2. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.

  3. Thread marinated lamb onto skewers, reserving any remaining marinade. Grill skewers until
    desired doneness, 10 to 12 minutes, basting with the reserved marinade and turning
    occasionally for even cooking.
